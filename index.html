Identity

Significant period of my development

At 17, landing my first job as a parcel repacker was thrilling, but it lasted only briefly. After a stint of idleness and socializing, my dreams of basketball stardom and academic pursuits dimmed due to financial setbacks. Despite feeling hopeless, I resolved to support my family, eventually securing a job as a dining staff member.

Just as hope for returning to school emerged, another obstacle arose, dashing my aspirations again. Desperation led me to a dishwasher role, which eventually opened doors to stable employment at a restaurant. With newfound opportunity and support from friends, I seized a scholarship for a diploma program called UAQTEA in ISHRM, reshaping my life's trajectory.

Balancing work and studies, I found purpose in providing for my family and myself. Through perseverance and the encouragement of loved ones, I now navigate life with maturity and determination, ever hopeful for a brighter future.

Function

My standard approach is to "go with the flow" when faced with unfamiliar or challenging situations. I find that instead of persistently fighting against the tide, I simply go with the current and enjoy all that life has to offer. I can easily and adaptably move through uncertainty thanks to this character, looking for opportunities within problems instead of fighting against them. I thrive in the versatility of now, believing that I can stay afloat amid life's waves, whether it is by finding clever solutions to challenging situations or adjusting to abrupt changes. Although I take great satisfaction in my capacity to adapt, this flexibility shows itself in more complex aspects of my personality when it comes to dealing with other people. Unfortunately, I've been known to have a toxic side and take things way too personally when I'm presented with negative feelings or experiences. My natural tendency in such situations is to strike back, frequently going against my moral convictions to make sure that perceived injustices receive proper compensation. I strive to overcome my urge towards revenge, which I acknowledge as a problem in myself. It's important to remember, though, that at my core, I am a good person who returns compassion for kindness given to me.
